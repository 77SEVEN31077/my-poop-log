Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers about using Poop Log Tracker. For deeper topics, see the Learn guides.

How do I log a bowel movement?

On the homepage, pick the date and time, then tap Log Poop. Your entry appears in the table with the interval since the previous record.

Where is my data stored?

All records stay in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is uploaded to a server. Clearing browser data or switching devices may remove your history.

Do I need an account?

No. The tool works immediately without sign-up, email, or login.

Can I back up my records?

There is no built-in export. You can screenshot or copy the table before clearing browser data if you want a manual backup.

What does the interval mean?

It shows how much time passed between one entry and the next older entry. It helps you notice spacing changes over time.

Can this tool diagnose anything?

No. It is for personal timing awareness only. See our Medical Disclaimer.

When should I talk to a doctor?

Contact a healthcare professional if you notice blood in stool, severe pain, persistent constipation or diarrhea, unexplained weight loss, or a sudden lasting change in bowel habits. See When to Talk to a Doctor for more detail.
