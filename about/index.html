Poop Log Tracker

About Poop Log Tracker

Independent · Privacy-first · General information only

Poop Log Tracker is a free, lightweight web tool that helps you record when bowel movements occur and review the time between entries. It runs entirely in your browser, stores log data locally on your device, and does not require an account.

Why the tool exists

Bowel habits are easy to misremember. When a clinician asks how long symptoms have lasted, many people guess from memory and lose useful detail. A simple timestamp log creates a factual timeline — when events happened and how spacing changed over days or weeks.

That timeline supports personal awareness during diet changes, travel, illness recovery, medication adjustments, hydration shifts, or stressful periods. It is not meant to replace medical judgment, but it can make self-observation and doctor conversations more concrete.

We built this site because existing options often add friction: sign-up walls, cloud sync, subscription prompts, or interfaces designed for broad health tracking rather than one focused task. Poop Log Tracker does one thing clearly.

Design principles

  • Minimal by default. Open the page, log a time, read your table. No onboarding funnel.
  • Local-first data. Your log entries stay in browser storage unless you choose to back them up manually.
  • No account gate. The tool works immediately without email, password, or profile setup.
  • Plain language. Explanatory content lives in Learn guides and FAQ, not on the tool homepage.
  • Honest scope. We describe what the tool can and cannot do, including medical limitations.
  • Readable on mobile. Layout and navigation work on phones as well as desktops.

Privacy-first approach

Bowel movement records are stored in your browser's localStorage. The website operator does not receive those entries on a server and does not operate a cloud database for your log.

Because storage is local, your history appears only on the device and browser where you created it. Clearing browser data, switching browsers, or changing devices can remove or separate your records. You can read more in our local storage guide and Privacy Policy.

The site may use Google Analytics for basic page-view measurement and Google AdSense for advertising. Analytics is configured for standard page views only — not for sending your log entries or health input. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How it works

  1. Select the hour and minute of your last bowel movement on the homepage.
  2. Click Log to save the entry.
  3. View your history and the interval since your previous record on the homepage.

You can clear all records at any time using Clear All Records. This version does not include a built-in export feature; manual screenshots or copies are the simplest backup method before clearing browser data.

What this tool does not do

Poop Log Tracker is for personal awareness only. It is not a medical device, diagnostic system, treatment planner, or emergency service. It does not analyze stool appearance, pain, blood, weight, nutrition, or hydration unless you track those details elsewhere.

It does not send alerts, predict disease, recommend medication, or connect you automatically to clinicians. Any medical decisions require evaluation by a qualified healthcare professional. See our Medical Disclaimer.

Who operates this site

Poop Log Tracker is independently maintained as a lightweight privacy-first tracking tool. The site is published at mypooplog.com and hosted as a static web project.
