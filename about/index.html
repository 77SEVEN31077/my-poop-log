About Poop Log Tracker

Poop Log Tracker is a lightweight browser tool for recording bowel movement times and seeing the interval between entries.

Why it exists

Most people do not need a full health app just to remember one simple thing: when did I last go? This tool keeps that task fast, private, and low-friction.

Privacy-first design

Records are stored in your browser. No account is required, and your log entries are not uploaded to a database operated by this site. Clearing browser data may delete your records.

What it does not do

Poop Log Tracker is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or emergency guidance. If you have concerning symptoms, talk to a qualified healthcare professional.

Operator

Poop Log Tracker is independently maintained as a lightweight privacy-first tracking tool. For feedback, privacy questions, or site issues, use the Contact page.
