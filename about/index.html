About Poop Log Tracker

Poop Log Tracker is a free, lightweight web tool that helps you keep track of when you last had a bowel movement and how much time passes between each one.

Why use it?

Regular bowel habits are a useful indicator of digestive health. Whether you're monitoring changes for personal awareness or simply curious about your patterns, this tool makes it easy to log times and see intervals at a glance — no spreadsheet required.

Completely private

Your records are stored only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded to a server, and no account is needed. Your data stays on your device.

How it works

  1. Select the time of your last bowel movement.
  2. Click Log to save the entry.
  3. View your history and the interval since your previous record in the table below.

You can clear all records at any time using the Clear All Records button.

Important note

This is a personal tracking tool, not a medical device or diagnostic service. If you have concerns about your digestive health, please consult a qualified healthcare professional. See our Medical Disclaimer for more details.
